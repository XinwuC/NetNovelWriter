# NAMING_GUIDE.md — Character & World Naming

Read this file during World Building and Character Development.

---

## Sources to Draw From (for inspiration, not direct copying)

**Classical texts:**
- 《山海经》— place names and creature names: 蓬莱, 昆仑, 烛龙, 相柳, 九尾, 帝江
- 《楚辞》— epithets, imagery, emotional register: 灵均, 云旗, 太息, 岁暮
- 《诗经》— natural imagery as names: 蒹葭, 在水, 伊人 (structural inspiration)
- 《聊斋志异》— character names are layered, often quietly ironic
- 《三侠五义》《儿女英雄传》— older wuxia name conventions, grounded and specific
- Historical courtesy names (字) as structural model: 子龙, 孟德, 玄德, 伯温

**Naming logic:**
- Names should carry meaning traceable to the character's fate, flaw, or hidden nature
- The character may not know what their name foreshadows
- Antagonist names often carry an ironic counter-meaning to their deeds

---

## Forbidden Patterns

**Character names — avoid these high-frequency xianxia defaults:**
- MC: 林风/林凡/陈浩/李明/云天/叶辰/萧炎/江流/楚天 and all variants
- Female leads: 冰雪/雪儿/紫萱/凌儿 type diminutives
- Elders/villains: 太上/玄冥/blood/bone prefix clichés

**Sect and organization names — avoid:**
- 天X宗/灵X宗/云X宗/苍X殿 without a distinctive grounding modifier
- Generic directional names: 东方剑派/北冥宫 unless tied to specific worldbuilding

**Technique names — avoid:**
- 天雷X/九阳X/万X诀/无极X — overused across thousands of novels
- Pure power-adjective stacking: 绝世无双毁天灭地X掌

## Construction Rules

**Characters:**
- Use two-character names with semantic tension: e.g., 凌迟 (execution method repurposed as name), 霜问 (frost + question)
- Single-character courtesy names are acceptable for elite characters: 玑, 渊, 璇
- Names from obscure classical plants, astronomical terms, or craft words feel distinct

**Sects / Organizations:**
- Ground in specific doctrine or founding event, not generic celestial imagery
- Format: [distinctive modifier] + [type]: 折枝盟, 问剑阁, 枯荣宗, 晚渡楼
- Avoid 宗, 门, 派 unless the sect's identity demands it; 阁, 楼, 盟, 司 feel fresher

**Techniques:**
- Unexpected pairings feel more real: 枯荣换血功, 折骨听雷步, 引尸还魂诀
- Name should imply the technique's cost or side effect, not just its power
- Avoid naming every technique — unnamed techniques feel more grounded

**Locations:**
- Use specific geography words: 渡口 (ferry crossing), 废墟 (ruins), 旱塬 (dry plateau)
- Layer history into place names: a sect built on a battlefield should have a name that references the battle
