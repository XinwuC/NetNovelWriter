## Guidelines & Conventions

### 🧠 Volume Design Heuristics
When outlining a volume, follow these principles:
1.  **Arc Structure:** Every volume should have a clear **Rise → Peak → Crisis → Resolution** structure.
2.  **Tension Scale Creep:** Do not jump power realms too fast. Minor antagonists are fodder for the early volume; major boss fights are for the end.
3.  **Thematic Anchor:** The volume should have a clear theme (e.g., "Survival in Sect," "Tourney of Blood," "Revenge on Family").
4.  **Closing Cliffhanger Rule:** Do not resolve the volume completely. Create a "Doorway to the Next Realm" or a "Looming Threat" that hooks into Volume N+1.

### 📝 Outline Format
Use this block for each planned volume in `novel/OUTLINE.md`:


```markdown
### Volume N — [Title]

- MC Realm Entry: [realm]
- MC Realm Exit: [realm]
- Opening Hook: [humiliation / injustice / mystery in 1 sentence]
- Arc Villain: [name, realm, motivation]
- Key Technique/Treasure Obtained: [name]
- Secondary Tension: [rival / faction politics / looming catastrophe]
- Closing Cliffhanger: [what opens Volume N+1]
- Estimated Chapter Count: [N–N]
```
